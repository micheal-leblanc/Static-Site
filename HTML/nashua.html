Welcome to Nashua, recognized twice as the "Best Place to Live in America" and a thriving New Hampshire economy.

Nashua was founded during the 19th century in the year 1853. Nashua's population is around 90,000 people and is located on the Southern border of New Hampshire. The average household income of Nashua is $91,000 which is higher than the state average. The city is classified as suburban, mostly due to its close proximity to Boston.

Downtown is known for its great resturants, vibrant shops and live music on Fridays.
