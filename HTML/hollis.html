Welcome to Hollis -- where a strong community and quality of life meets rural charm.

Hollis was founded in 1746 and currently holds a population of aproximately 8500 people. Hollis is known as a rural farm town located close to the New Hampshire and Massachusetts border in the souther part of the state. The average household income of Hollis residents is $120,000.

Stop by any of our local farms for fresh farm-to-table products today!
