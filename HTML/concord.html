Welcome to the State Capital!

Concord is the capital city of New Hamshire and was founded in 1734. Featuring both urban and suburban elements, Concord is a quick 30 minute drive south of the White Mountains where around 45,000 people call Concord their home. The average household is approximately $85,000 annually which is a typical income for cities located in the Southern part of the state.

One distinctive feature is the gold-plated dome of the State Capital building.
